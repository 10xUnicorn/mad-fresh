## Description (4000 chars max)

Mad Fresh Kitchen is Arizona's premier meal prep and delivery app. Order fresh, customizable protein bowls, wraps, salads, and more — delivered weekly or available for same-day pickup in Tempe, AZ.

FRESH MEALS, YOUR WAY
Browse our chef-crafted menu and build your perfect bowl. Choose your protein, sides, and sauces. Every meal is made fresh — never frozen, never microwaved.

WEEKLY MEAL PREP SUBSCRIPTIONS
Save time and money with automatic weekly deliveries. Choose from 5, 10, or 15 meals per week in Small, Medium, or Large portions. Founding members get exclusive pricing locked in forever.

REWARDS & LOYALTY
Earn points on every order. Unlock free meals, merch packs, and exclusive perks as you level up from Seedling to Legend. Maintain your weekly streak for bonus rewards.

QUICK REORDER
One tap to reorder your favorites. Your most-ordered meals are always front and center so you never have to browse if you don't want to.

CORPORATE CATERING
Feeding a team? Mad Fresh handles corporate catering and school lunch programs across the Phoenix metro area. Request a quote directly in the app.

GIVE BACK
Donate meals to those in need through our community donation program. Track your impact and earn bonus loyalty points for your generosity.

KEY FEATURES:
• Chef-crafted bowls, wraps, salads, sides, and desserts
• Customizable protein and side selections
• Weekly subscription meal prep (5/10/15 meals)
• Same-day pickup in Tempe, AZ
• Valley-wide delivery
• Loyalty rewards and achievement badges
• One-tap reorder from order history
• Referral program — earn 500 points per friend
• Community meal donations
• Corporate catering and school lunch programs
• Secure payments via Stripe
